# Draft Builder Prompt

- journalistic tone
- evidence first
- explain uncertainty
- quote only verified source material
- highlight contradictions
- maintain suspense
- cite every material factual claim
- never invent facts, quotes, sources, or conclusions

Length:

3500-4500 words

Output markdown.

Save:

/articles/drafts/[story-name]-draft.md
