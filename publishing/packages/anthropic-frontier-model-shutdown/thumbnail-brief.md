# Thumbnail Brief - Anthropic Frontier Model Shutdown

## Primary Concept

A real, recognizable Claude interface or Anthropic product screen displaying an unavailable-access state, paired with a photographed U.S. Department of Commerce building or official federal document imagery.

Overlay: **AI SHUT DOWN**

Emotional trigger: sudden loss of access.

Curiosity trigger: who ordered it and why?

Style: investigative documentary, high contrast, restrained red warning accent, no generic robot imagery.

## Alternate Concepts

1. Calendar pages marked June 9 and June 12 with Fable 5 between them. Overlay: **3 DAYS ONLINE**
2. Redacted government letter over an authentic Anthropic interface. Overlay: **THE SECRET ORDER**
3. Split screen: "FOREIGN NATIONALS" on the directive side and "EVERYONE BLOCKED" on the product side.
4. U.S. passport silhouettes over a cloud-access screen. Overlay: **AI BY NATIONALITY?**
5. Project Glasswing infrastructure imagery going dark. Overlay: **DEFENDERS CUT OFF**

## Accuracy Rules

- Do not fabricate the actual directive.
- Any document used must be clearly labeled as an illustration unless it is authentic.
- Do not depict an explosion, attack, or breach that did not occur.
- Do not state that the government directly ordered a worldwide ban.
