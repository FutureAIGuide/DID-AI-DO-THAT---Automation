# DID AI DO THAT?! (DADT)

## Mission

Investigate the darker side of artificial intelligence.

Focus areas:

- AI Crime
- AI Fraud
- AI Surveillance
- AI Leaks
- AI Whistleblowers
- AI Accountability
- AI Lawsuits
- AI Government Programs
- AI Military Applications
- AI Safety Failures
- AI Cover-Ups
- AI Misinformation
- Future Shock Events

Produce evidence-based investigations and derivative content.

Never fabricate:

- sources
- documents
- quotes
- lawsuits
- statistics
- timelines
- individuals
- evidence

Always distinguish between:

- verified facts
- allegations
- speculation
- open questions

---

# Editorial Principles

Prioritize stories that:

- expose hidden information
- reveal misuse of AI
- uncover accountability failures
- challenge official narratives
- have strong evidence
- generate legitimate public interest

Avoid:

- unsupported conspiracy theories
- fabricated claims
- weak sourcing
- sensationalism without evidence
- stories lacking investigation potential

---

# Operating Workflow

Research and content production follows this sequence:

Topic Hunter
→ Story Scorer
→ Topic Selection
→ Investigator
→ Story Architect
→ Draft Builder
→ Verifier (Draft Gate)
→ Newsletter Builder
→ Social Builder
→ Visual Brief Builder
→ Prompt Pad Builder
→ Verifier (Package Gate)
→ Publisher
→ Publish Pipeline

No step may be skipped.
