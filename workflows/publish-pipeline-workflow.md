# Publish Pipeline

Act as COO of DID AI DO THAT?!

Objective:

Transform completed investigations into a fully packaged publishing bundle.

Inputs:

/case-files/published
/articles/final
/newsletter/final
/social/linkedin
/social/x-threads
/video/shorts
/video/tiktoks
/assets/thumbnails

For every completed investigation:

STEP 1 — VALIDATION

Verify:

- sources exist
- citations exist
- timeline is coherent
- legal claims are sourced
- article exceeds minimum word count
- newsletter exists
- social assets exist
- video assets exist

Flag missing items.

Save report:

/publishing/reports

------------------------------------------------

STEP 2 — CONTENT PACKAGING

Create:

Publishing Package

Include:

- final article
- newsletter
- LinkedIn posts
- X threads
- Shorts scripts
- TikTok scripts
- thumbnail briefs
- prompt pad

Save:

/publishing/packages/[story-name]

------------------------------------------------

STEP 3 — SEO PACKAGE

Generate:

- SEO title
- GEO title
- meta description
- slug
- tags
- categories
- schema suggestions

Save:

/publishing/packages/[story-name]/seo.md

------------------------------------------------

STEP 4 — YOUTUBE PACKAGE

Generate:

- title options
- description
- chapter suggestions
- tags
- thumbnail recommendations

Save:

/publishing/packages/[story-name]/youtube.md

------------------------------------------------

STEP 5 — NEWSLETTER PACKAGE

Generate:

- subject lines
- preview text
- featured image suggestions
- CTA variations

Save:

/publishing/packages/[story-name]/newsletter.md

------------------------------------------------

STEP 6 — DISTRIBUTION PLAN

Generate:

Publication Day

+0 hours:
Main article

+2 hours:
LinkedIn Post #1

+4 hours:
X Thread #1

+8 hours:
Short #1

+24 hours:
Newsletter

+48 hours:
Follow-up Thread

+72 hours:
Second Short

Save:

/publishing/packages/[story-name]/distribution.md

------------------------------------------------

STEP 7 — ARCHIVE

Move completed story assets to:

/archive/[story-name]

Retain original source material.

------------------------------------------------

STEP 8 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Create:

Story Name
Publication Status
Asset Count
Distribution Schedule
Future Follow-Ups

Save:

/publishing/executive-summaries/[story-name]-executive-summary.md
