# Daily Discovery Workflow

1. Discover 50 emerging AI stories.

2. Cluster them into:

- AI Crime
- AI Surveillance
- AI Leaks
- AI Cover-Ups
- AI Fraud
- AI Government
- AI Accountability

3. Save discovery results to `/research/incoming`.
4. Score all candidates and save results to `/research/scored`.
5. Return the top 10 opportunities.
6. Select one top story and save the decision to `/research/selected`.
7. Generate one investigation packet in `/case-files/active`.

Use the full production pipeline only after the selected story passes each gate.
